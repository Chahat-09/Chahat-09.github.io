Stock Markets Show Volatility Amid Global Tensions

Investors remain cautious as markets fluctuate worldwide

By Chahat Sharma | March 18, 2026 | New York, USA

Updated 2 hours ago

Stock markets across the globe experienced significant volatility as investors reacted to economic uncertainty and geopolitical developments.

Major indices showed mixed performance, with technology stocks facing pressure while banking sectors remained relatively stable.

Experts suggest that inflation concerns and global instability continue to impact investor sentiment.
