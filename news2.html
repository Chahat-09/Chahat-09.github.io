Urban Flooding Raises Questions About City Planning

March 2026

Heavy rainfall in recent days has caused widespread waterlogging, disrupting transportation and daily life in urban areas.

The situation highlights gaps in drainage systems and poor infrastructure planning in rapidly growing cities.

Experts suggest long-term solutions including improved drainage design and sustainable urban development policies.

The issue emphasizes the need for cities to become more resilient to environmental changes.
