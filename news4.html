New Education Policy Brings Major Changes

Focus shifts toward skill-based and flexible learning models

Date: March 5, 2026 | Location: New Delhi, India

India’s new education policy aims to modernize the learning system through flexible and skill-based approaches.

The policy promotes digital education, practical knowledge, and interdisciplinary learning.

Experts believe these reforms will better prepare students for future careers.
